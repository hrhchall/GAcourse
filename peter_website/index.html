Hello

I'm a freelance photographer specialising in landscape and still life.

Based in Devon in the South West of England, I draw inspiration from the surrounding countryside and enjoy capturing nature's patterns and intricacies.

As well as photographing my local area, I'm also a keen traveller and many of my photographs were taken on holidays in Italy and Spain. My tastes are eclectic, but my most recent work veers towards monochrome. Many of my pics are taken with a compact, point and shoot (Sony DSC-T5 & Sony DSC-HX9V).

Please don't hesitate to drop me a line - I'm always happy to discuss a project, however big or small.

If you would like more information, advice, an estimate or to enquire about buying prints
of an image you have seen on the site, please get in touch.
