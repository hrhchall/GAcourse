Undergraduates

Are you a 2nd year, science undergraduate looking for something fun, challenging and rewarding to do that will be totally different from your studies?

Might you enjoy an opportunity to share your knowledge and enthusiasm with younger science students? Are you interested in making yourself stand out from the crowd, in the eyes of employers?

If so, Pro Science may be the ideal opportunity for you. Open the informational leaflet below, that corresponds to your University, to find out what you'd do as a member of Pro Science and how you could get involved.



What we look for

We don't expect you to have lots of teaching experience (although sometimes this can prove very useful), but we absolutely do expect, from the word go:

  1. Commitment and reliability
  2. Initiative and a self-starting attitude
  3. Great people skills and team-working
  4. Strong communication skills

Some placements will be more challenging than others and we are particularly looking for those of you who are keen to really challenge yourselves. Working with some classes and specific pupils will require a lot of patience and resilience at times.

We want to work with confident individuals, who will strive to gain the respect of their pupils and who will act as positive role-models for them.

Support our work

Please contact us if you feel you can support us in our mission. Email: katie@proscience.co.uk

Whatever the type of support you have in mind - be it financial, support or in kind, in an advisory role or through partnering with us - helping with training or the developing of our programme, we would hugely appreciate hearing from you.

We know that working with others is a key part to us achieving our objectives and we are constantly looking for new ways to do this. Please do forward on our details if you think of someone that we should be talking with!
