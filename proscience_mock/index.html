What we do

Pro Science empowers exceptional STEM undergraduates to champion the sciences in schools, focussing on schools in socio-economically disadvantaged areas.

Our Pro Science undergraduates:

  1. Act as STEM role models who deliver novel STEM activities for pupils on a frequent, sustained basis throughout the school year;
  2. Support teachers to deliver curriculum objectives and meet their professional development requirements;
  3. Build and strengthen industry links that inform the content of our activities and which connect employers to their local communities;

Why we exist

This problem is especially prominent in schools in socio-economically deprived areas.
