Our vision

A world where a future in science, technology, engineering and maths is accessible to every young person, regardless of their social background.

Aims and Objectives

  1. Make a measurable improvement to pupils’ enjoyment of STEM subjects;
  2. Make a measurable improvement to pupils’ perceptions of the opportunities STEM can lead to;
  3. Make a measurable improvement to the number of pupils choosing to pursue STEM subjects at various levels of education......;
  4. ...Impact on Undergraduates;
  5. ...Proven as useful link for companies to build relationships with UGs;

Where we're working

Over the last two years, our volunteers have been working in six schools in Edinburgh. This year we are working with ten.

  • Trinity Academy
  • St Augustine's RC High School
  • Stewart's Melville College
  • Firhill High School
  • Broughton High School
  • Tynecastle High School
  • Craigmount High School
  • Liberton High School
