Pasaporte Cultural is a social enterprise that is transforming the experience of language learning in schools by fusing music and Spanish in a totally innovative way


  • We believe that in order to foster a love of languages students should be exposed to the language and culture in an authentic context
  • We design and deliver immersive culture workshops led by native speakers in 100% target language in which students write and perform their own compositions alongside professional musicians
  • At Pasaporte Cultural we believe that contextualising a language is key to motivation. For many students, going abroad is not an option. We bring the culture to the classroom and recreate the experience of immersion learning.
  • The combination of culture, performance and careers sets students up for life as confident and competent linguists who are enthusiastic about using this essential skill to better their futures
Pasaporte workshops foster a love of languages by giving students the opportunity to connect with the culture and people of the Spanish speaking world.
