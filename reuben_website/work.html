I explore issues arising at the intersection of openness and privacy, identifying the tensions between them, and how they can be mutually reinforcing.

Through developing tools and best practices, I hope to make it easier for data subjects to exercise their rights, and for data controllers to meet their obligations. Find out more

View some of my academic papers and resources.

I research the emerging landscape of personal data empowerment; organisations, tools and initiatives that enable people to control and benefit from their own data.

I'm a member of the Creative Commons UK affiliate team. We build and help maintain communities of CC license users in the UK. Find out more

I try to help create open standards and infrastructures for Privacy Policies and Terms of Service, so we can make them machine and human-readable. I'm a co-founder of Open Notice and a member of Kantara ISWG.
